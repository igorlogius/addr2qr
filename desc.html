Display a url as qrcode for easy sharing between devices with qrcode scanner. Get the url of the currently displayed tab as a QR Code for easy sharing (scanning) for mobile devices like smartphones. Usage:
  1. Open any real url (no firefox internal pages e.g. about:config, newtab, ... )
  2. Click on the pageAction icon on the right side of the url bar
  3. Scan the QRCode from the Popup window below the icon.
Notes:

    1. "activeTab", used for accessing the active tab url
